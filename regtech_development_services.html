Features Of Strong RegTech Product

In the Regtech industry, technology is utilised to disrupt regulations. In simple words, Regtech can be considered as a tool to solve compliance and regulatory issues. Using Regtech software and solutions, a company can identify and predict the lending risks in a few seconds. It automates the tasks of analysts in the form of Customer Due Diligence, Anti Money Laundering and Entity Investigation. This helps to save time since manual labour involved in an investigation can be reduced which leads to increase in efficiency.

Blockchain created a revolution in Regtech industries because of their increased transparency, improved record-keeping and enhanced security through cryptography.

Agility

Developed product should allow transfer and conversion of complex data and into ready to use information.

Speed

Quick configuration and generation of reports is crucial in all Regtech company .

Integrations

Easy customizable features, powerful integrations and ability to comply with regulatory changes are essential in all Regtech software.

Elements Of RegTech

Conducting systematic tests in an intended way is possible by arranging regulations, laws, or principles according to a plan

Assessment of risk through compliance testing and recommending solutions based on analysed data or set regulations

Determination and management of data like (KYC,EM,IM) within the context of given risk area

Tracking dynamic data and other analytics data as source of potential risk

Advantages Of Blockchain Technology In RegTech Industry

Speeds up the client onboarding process

Track frauds and prevents money laundering activities

Faster adaptation to new regulations

Effective data collection and utilisation

Improved data analytics

Use-Cases Of Blockchain In Reg-Tech

Regtech company utilises blockchain for the following use cases

AML and KYC Regulations

Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and KYC regulations which need to be updated regularly for existing counterparties are the most important use cases for blockchain apps in RegTech industry

Monitoring

Blockchain ensures better traceability and quicker analysis of digitized assets. It helps to monitor and prevent the money laundering in virtual currencies.

Record Keeping

Regtech firms apply blockchain to create transparency in data , a distributed ledger internal use, data protection and documentation for audit purposes .

RFM

Assets tokenization is an example of blockchain technology in the Regtech industry.
