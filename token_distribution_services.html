The crypto token is a virtual cryptocurrency that represents a tradable asset using blockchain technology. Tokens increase the amount of money that may be raised through digital assets. Our knowledgeable developers can assist you in developing and launching a brand new blockchain token from scratch.

Types Of Digital Tokens

  • Stable Token
  • Security Token
  • Asset Token
Stable Token

Due to the extreme price volatility of nearly all digital tokens, there is a growing demand for tokens with some form of value anchoring or internal stabilizing mechanism embedded, in order to cater to new users who are unwilling to accept the current levels of risk associated with holding digital tokens.

Security Token

Security tokens are digital, liquid contracts for fractions of any existing asset with value, like real estate, a car, or company stock. When investors use security tokens, they can expect their ownership share to be kept on the blockchain ledger.

Asset Token

Asset-backed tokens, for those unfamiliar, are blockchain-based units of value that are linked to real-world assets like corporate shares, real estate, diamonds, or commodities. As a result, asset-backed tokens are a crucial sort of token since they represent real-world assets.

Blockchains for Token Platform

The development of blockchain-based apps is enabled by blockchain platforms. They might be either permissioned or unpermitted. Ethereum, Hyperledger, R3, Ripple, and EOS are just a handful of the blockchain frameworks that individuals can use to create and host applications on the blockchain.

Let's start with the criteria that can help you pick which blockchain platform is appropriate for your company.

Binance

Binance is a well-known cryptocurrency exchange that operates in the cryptocurrency industry. It has a huge trading volume for Litecoin, Ethereum, and Tron, among other cryptocurrencies. The Binance Chain was created specifically for Binance. It's also our most recent product of interest.

Litecoin

Litecoin, like other cryptocurrencies, is a global, digital payment system that employs blockchain technology to transfer and trade "Coins." The litecoin blockchain is the world's largest crypto-based network.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized, open-source blockchain that allows users to create smart contracts. The platform's native cryptocurrency is Ether. After bitcoin, it is the second-largest cryptocurrency in terms of market capitalization.

Tron

Tron is a decentralised blockchain-based platform that aspires to create a free, global digital content entertainment system using distributed storage technology and enables for easy and cost-effective digital content distribution.
